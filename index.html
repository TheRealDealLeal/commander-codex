About Extra Life

Extra Life is an annual charity gaming marathon benefiting Children's Miracle Network Hospitals. Participants raise money and then play games for 24 hours to support their local children's hospital. Since 2008, Extra Life has raised over $100 million to help kids and families in need. Our pod has been proud to participate since 2022, and we grow our fundraising every single year.
